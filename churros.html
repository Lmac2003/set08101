Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup flour
  • Oil for frying
  • Sugar & cinnamon for coating
  • Chocolate sauce (optional)

(Click to see steps)

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil water, sugar, and oil together.
  2. Add flour and stir until a dough forms.
  3. Let cool slightly, then pipe into hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Roll in cinnamon sugar.
  6. Serve warm with chocolate sauce!
